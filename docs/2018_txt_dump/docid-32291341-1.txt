IY Re Miami letter to Bureau, dated 8/9/63.

. During. the month of August, 1963, the following
interviews were conducted by Agents of the Miami Office in
connection with captioned program,

: With reference to SAMUEL M, GIANCANA, Miami file
92-170, Bufile 92-3171, on 8/19/63, SAs JAMES J, KEARNEY and
FRANK J, SMITH interviewed BEN W. SWIFT, JR., 1832 South
Ocean Drive, Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, SWIFT, through his
association with CHRISTINE MC GUIRE has developed a personal
relationship with SAMUEL M. GIANCANA, commissionéd member of
La Cosa Nostra.

 

. SWIFT was receptive to being interviewed and has
agreed to advise Agent KEARNEY of GIANCANA's whereabouts and
activities when in the Greater Miami, Florida, area, It is
anticipated that the Miami Office, supplied with information
from this source will be in a position to provide discreet
coverage of GIANCANA in this area,

SWIFT related that he was in GIANCANA's company
recently in Las Vegas, Nevada, and observed that "celebrities"
DEAN MARTIN, FRANK SINATRA, and EDDIE FISHER were friendly
with GIANCANA and treated him as a "big shot." He recalled
that FISHER came over to GIANCANA's table and was most cordial
to him. He said that GIANCANA, referring to the recent fuling
of a Chicago court, said that he was pleased and satisfied that
he made the Bureau "cop the fifth." ! °
