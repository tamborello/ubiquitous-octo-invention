Reference is made to report of Special Agent THOMAS G.
FORSYTH, III, Pittsburgh, November 14, 1962.

There follows a brief characterization of informants
used in referenced report.

PG T-3 is a professional man whose services have on

occasion been used by members of the MANNARINO organization

and who frequents MANNARINO controlled gambling and
after-hours establishments.

PG T-4 is a businessman whose business activities bring
him into occasional contact with members of the —
racketeering element in New Kensington, Pa.

PG T-24 is a life-long resident of New Kensington
who has for many years held a low echelon position
in the rackets.

PG T-27 is a New Kensington Businessman and minor
politician with wide acquaintance in the racketeering
element of the area.

PG T-39 is a Pittsburgh, Pa., gambler and club operator.
