PURPOSE: To discuss changes in the staffing of two facets of the
JFK Task Force: the processing of documents and the production
of the Identification Aid (IA).

SYNOPSIS: Instant memorandum explains the need to reconfigure
the JFK Task Force. The Oswald file team would be reduced from
14 people to six people. The team working on the JFK
Assassination file would be reduced from 20 people to nine
people. The team producing IAs would be enlarged from eight to
23 people.

RECOMMENDATION: None. For information.
