Re Miami airtel 9/30/59 and Bulet 10/12/59.
HUGO GONZALEZ LAJONCHERE en ‘November 17, 1959, advised

he had been in touch witby)SERGIO SANJENIS CABARROCAS and explained

to SANJENIS that. ROLANDO WASFERRER desired to have SANJENIS con-
tact him. GONZALEZ LAJONCHERE stated he had informed SANJENIS
concerning MASFERRER's desires and that SANJENIS did intend to
contact MASFERRER. GONZALEZ LAJONCHERE pointed out that he be-
lieves that SANJENIS definitely will not partake of any counter-
revolutionary plot in which MASFERRER is involved. GONZALEZ
LAJONCHERE stated that it was his belief that SANJENIS. might
give some indication to MASFERRER of cooperating with him but
that he doubted very much whether SANJENIS would become involved
in any counterrevolutionary movement headed by MASFERRER. GON-
ZALEZ, LAJONCHERE stated, however, that he did not belief that
SANJENIS would inform Cuban authorities concerning anything he
might learn from MASFERRER,
