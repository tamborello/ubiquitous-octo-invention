The following information regarding the subject's activities
in Vienna, Austria, was furnished under date of 2/5/59 by Mix. JOSEF HOLAUBEK,

, .

GUS ALEX, born 1/4/14, in Chicago, American citizen, and.his
wife, MARIANNE RYAN ALEX, arrived in Vienna, Austria, on 1/6/59 and registered
in the Hotel Imperial at 16 Kaerntnerring, Vienna I, Austria. ALEX was
carrying United States passport #730.270 good until 7/12/59. Upon arrival
at the hotel, he listed his permanent address as 9353 South Spaulding
Avenue, Evergreen Park, Illinois, USA. His double room at the hotel had
been reserved by the Drake Travel Service through the "Cosmos" travel service
in Vienna.

Personnel at the Hotel Imperial were shown photographs of the
subject and his wife and were able to readily identify these photographs.
They, however, could not furnish any information indicating any unusual
activities on the part of either the subject or his wife while they remained
at the hotel. The couple appeared to engage in the usual sight-seeing and ~
shopping activities in Vienna and there was no information that they had re-
ceived any guests at the hotel. Both on 1/8 and on 1/9, the subject placed
telephone calls to the Hotel "Baur" in Zurich, Switzerland.

The subject and his wife departed Vienna on 1/9/59 for an Gees
