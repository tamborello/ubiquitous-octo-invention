SANTO TRAFFICANTE, JR.

I. Personal History and Background

A. Date and Place of Birth

Santo Trafficante, Jr., was born November 15, 1914,
at Tampa, Florida, to Santo Trafficante, Sr., and Maria
Giuseppe Cacciatore, who were both born in Italy.

B. Education

Santo Trafficante, Jr., entered George Washington
Junior High School, Tampa, Florida, on February 14, 1927,
at which time his parent was listed as Santo Trafficante,
1718 Michigan Avenue, Tampa, Florida. In September, 1929,
Trafficante entered Hillsborough Senior High School, Tampa,
Florida. Trafficante only attended Hillsborough Senior High
School for one semester and in the tenth grade left school.
