Referenced report of SA CLABURN T. WHITE contains
information concerning crime conditions at Hot Springs, Ark.

The period shown in this report reflects dates of
investigation and file reviews since receipt of Bureau airtel.
The dates of previous interviews and investigation conducted
are not included in period of this report.

Extensive investigation has been conducted at Hot
Springs, Arkansas, concerning the gambling activity to
determine if those individuals involved are in violation. of
the new Federal legislation regarding interstate gambling
activities.

Cases have been opened on the three larger gambling
clubs in relation to cashing of out-of-state checks at casinos
and investigation instituted as the USA, Fort Smith, Ark., has
advised this may be in violation of Federal law,

There was an influx of casino employees to Hot
Springs from other states seeking employment in the casinos
during the horse race meet at Hot Springs which commenced on
2/17/62. Numerous cases have been opened on these individuals
traveling interstate. The USA has indicated this is possibly
a violation,
