Postl's Health Club
188 West Randolph Street

CG T-40 advised that ALEX was at Postl's Health
Club every day during the week of April 16 to 20, 1962.
CG T-40 advised that. ALEX visits the club at least two
or three times a week and that ALEX is given exercises by
CHARLES POSTL, owner 'of Postl's Health Club. CG T-40
further advised that ALEX was instructed by his doctor
to visit Postl"s Health Club regularly and to take the
exercises given to him by POSTL to quiet his nerves.

with Illinois registration 520925, This registration was
determined to be listed to CHARLES ZERAVICH, jr., 2422 Clybourn
Avenue, It was determined that CHARLES ZERAVICH in addition
