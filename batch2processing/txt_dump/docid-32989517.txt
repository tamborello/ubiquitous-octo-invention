HOUSTUDY 75
Re Bureau nitel to all sacs, 6/13/75.

The Bureau has advised that the code name "Houstudy 75"
has been designated for all matters relating to House Select
Committee To Study Governmental Operations with respect to
intelligence activities, and the Bureau's handling of matters
pertaining thereto. Use Bureau file 62-116464, and above
caption for matters relating to House Committee as separate
from Senstudy 75, for matters relating to the Senate Committee.

You will recall that the FBI has pledged full
cooperation with these committees, however, we have an
obligation that sensitive sources and methods are fully
protected. If contacted by any member of this committee,
you are to call attention to this matter to the SAC, who
will contact Legal Counsel for guidance.
