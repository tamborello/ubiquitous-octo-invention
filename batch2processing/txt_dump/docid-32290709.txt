Synopsis:

 

Investigation discloses Agrupacion Montecristi (AM) not (OFK)
known to be active in Boston, Washington, D. C., and Newark
areas. In 8/62, the Directorio Revolucionaric Estudiantil

(DRE), an anti-CASTRO organization, combined with approxi-

mately four members of AM in carrying out naval action

against Cuban target, resulting in shelling of Havana hotel.
Miami source reports AM receiving some financial aid from

Consejo Revolucionario.Cubano (Cuban Revolutionary Council)
(CRC); organization currently inactive and awaiting some

form of aid from U. S. government.
