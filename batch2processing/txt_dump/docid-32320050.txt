Details: (U) The John F. Kennedy Assassination Records
Collection Act of 1992 mandated that all federal agencies process
their records related to the assassination and provide all the
original documents to the National Archives for public review.
The FBI has been working with the ARRB established by this act to
complete the project. Other ARRB employees have access to the
FBI building to accomplish this job.

(U) The ARRB, advised the JFKTF that Ms. Fagnant would
be reviewing FBI records in the JFKTF space and will need access
to the FBI building on a daily basis. She has her Top Secret
clearance and should be allowed access to the FBI's JFKTF. On
6/23/97, the ARRB advised telephonically that Ms. Fagnant would
begin reviewing documents 6/24/97, due to the limited time left
for completion of the project. The JFK Task Force requests that
the review of her clearance be expedited, so the Board’s or the
Task Force’s work is not disrupted for an extended period of
time.
