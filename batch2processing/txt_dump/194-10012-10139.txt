SITUATION :- AMS Series M 841, Sheet 4852
BAUTZEN MR 589690

LOWE Products :-

Passenger carriages, railway goods and tipper
wagons, refrigerator wagons and coach building
for omnibuses. Sidelines were furniture, prams
and metal household goods.
