"12/9/63

"On 12/6/63 a Buffalo Branch Workers
World Party (BB WWP) meeting was held at
headquarters 831 Main Street, Buffalo, N.Y.
Ernie Weissman was Chairman, Helen Richardson,
Recording Secretary, and Loretta McNiff,
Financial Secretary. MceNiff reported balance
on hand of $47.81,

"Helen Richardson read a communication
from Sam Ballan. regarding a special edition of
‘Workers World,! which concerned the assassination
of President Kennedy.. In this communication
Ballan mentioned that National Headquarters of
WWP plans to put out other special editions, if
necessary, to keep the people advised of current
events. .

-"Milton Neidenberg gave the Organizer's
report. He said that the Cuban Hurricane Relief
meeting scheduled for 11/23/63 at the Richford
Hotel, Buffalo, at which V. T, Lee from New York
City, National Chairman of the Fair Play for Cuba
Committee, had been scheduled to speak, was
canceled due to the assassination of President
Kennedy, and as they did not know what public
opinion would be if the meeting was held as
scheduled, and they did not want any trouble.
