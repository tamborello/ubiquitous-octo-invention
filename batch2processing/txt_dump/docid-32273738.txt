Dear Bert:

This is in response to your letter of June 25, 1975, concerning the
request of Senator Frank Church, Chairman of the Senate Select
Committee to Study Governmental Operations with Respect to
Intelligence Activities, for permission for certain members of the
Committee staff to examine classified defense material which is now
among the Presidential papers in the John F. Kennedy Library.

You are hereby authorized to grant access to classified defense mate-
rial in the files identified in the enclosure to your letter by the follow-
ing Committee staff members each of whom currently holds security
clearances as listed:

Peter Fenn, TS/SI/TK/B

Karl R. Inderfurth, TS/SI/TK/B
Alan Rombert, TS/SI/TK/B
Gregory F.Treverton, TS/SI/TK/B

Please be advised that the classified defense material of concern must
be protected as is required for material of the classification involved
and that declassification review by the Department of Defense is re-
quired prior to public release, if any, of such material in whole or in
part. The Department of Defense interposes no objection to your
acceding to Committee requests for copies of documents containing
classified defense material provided the copies are brought under control
and afforded security protection as prescribed for safeguarding
