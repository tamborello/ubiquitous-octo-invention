Synopsis: Results of requested searches regarding Clint
Murchison, Sr., Clint Murchison, Jr.,-H.L. Hunt, Nelson Bunker
Hunt, Lamar Hunt, and Paul M. Rothermel, Jr. for the period 1960
through 1969.

Enclosures: Dallas main files 62-3069 and’105-1484 and control
BQ file serial 92-99-SubI-9.

Details: A full search of manual and automated General Indices,
Confidential Indices, CIMS and IIS of the Dallas Division
provided negative results regarding Clint Murchison, Sr., H.L.
Hunt, Nelson Bunker Hunt, and Lamar Hunt for the period 1960
through 1969. The same searches provided positive results for
Clint Murchison, Jr. and Paul M. Rothermel, Jr. Murchison appears
in file 62-3069 (provided in its entirety of 75 serials) and 92-
99-SubI-9. Rothermel appears in file 105-1484 (provided in its
entirety of four serials) and 92-99-SubI-9(consisting of one
serial).
