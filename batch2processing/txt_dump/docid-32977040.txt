Agent(s) making the Survey, will be attached to the Final Survey
Report. Lo

Also attached are copies of an "Information Outline" for use

if desired by Local Enforcement Agencies in making Surveys re-

garding Presidential Protection. It is very important that this

Outline be made available for use of other agencies, if they wish
to use it. Under no circumstances should it be indicated that

this Service wants. it to be used, and no suggestion to use it
should be made. The decision to use it will rest with the other
agency, If it is to be used, make it clear in briefings that
this Outline is very general and covers only some of the factors
involved in any Security Survey. It is intended only as a guide~
line and is not expected to meet all, or even many, of the
situations likely to be encountered in a Survey. —
