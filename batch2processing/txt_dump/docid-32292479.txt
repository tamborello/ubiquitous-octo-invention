SUZANNE FUEGER arrived at Munich by air at 11:00AM
11/23/64, coming from Paris, France. She was met at Munich-
Riem Airport by her mother, ELROSA FUEGER, whose residence
address is House #1, Riederau, District of Landsberg/Lech, near
Munich. The two women entered a beige colored Volkswagen
automobile with a sliding roof bearing license number 11-LJ-69
and proceeded from the airport to Hotel Koenigshof am Stachus
in Munich. There FUEGER and her mother engaged room 512 for
three days. They remained in the hotel until 2:00 PM when
they left and walked along the adjoining streets. During
this time they entered the banking institution, Bayerische
Hypo-Bank located on the Stachus. They were in this bank for
only a short while and the reason for.their call has as yet
not been determined, They returned to the hotel at 4:10PM.
