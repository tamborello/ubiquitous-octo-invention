ADMINISTRATIVE DATA:

The Miami Division and WFO have continued to
develop information concerning the whereabouts and
associations of subject JOSEPH FRANCIS NESLINE, in the
case entitled "JOSEPH FRANCIS NESLINE; Unknown Wire Service,
AR; IGA - WIRE SERVICE, 00:WFO, BUfile 92-3000." This
information is not being repeated herein since captioned
matter pertains only to the arrest of NESLINE, TOURINE
and associates by state authorities in St. Mary's County,
Maryland anc their subsequent indictment by Federal
Grand Jury in Washington, D.C. on 5/13/65, for violation
of Federal and District of Columbia gambling statutes.

During the course of the current investigation
the following Baltimore confidential informants were
contacted but were unable to furnish any pertinent
information concerning NESLINE or TOURINE:
