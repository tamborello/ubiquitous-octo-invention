- Mhis is to acknowledge your letter of 16 August 1995
to Director of Central Intelligence Deutch describing the
Assassination Records Review Board's most recent formal
determinations with respect. to CIA records.

The appropriate Agency components have again reviewed
the 37 documents which the Board has decided to release,
either fully or with some information postponed. Please
advise the Board that the Agency does not plan to appeal
to the President for additional postponements with respect
to the documents listed in your letter.

We understand that further review will be needed by
December 1995 to determine if additional information in a
number of the documents can be released. We would be pleased
to discuss with the Board and your staff any issues that may
arise with regard to declassifying and releasing CIA-originated

information.
