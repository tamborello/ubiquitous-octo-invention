Reference is made to the report of Special Agent
THOMAS G. FORSYTH, III, Pittsburgh, dated July 26, 1963.

There follows a brief characterization of informants
used in referenced report.

PG T-2 is an individual who has had wide activities
for at least twenty years in the fields of prostitution and
gambling in the NewKensington, Pa., area.

PG T-4 is a Pittsburgh, Pa., business man, whose
business activities frequently bring him!tin contact with
racketeers in the New Kensington, Pa., area,

PG T-32 is a business man in Mercer County, Pa.,
who has a life-time association with gamblers and racketeers.

- PG T-35 is an individual who has worked in numerous
racket setups in Westmoreland and Allegheny Counties, both
Pennsylvania.

PG T-39 is a gambler and club operator in Pittsburgh,
Pa.

PG T-49 is an individual who is engaged in a
legitimate enterprise and who has resided in the McKeesport
area all of his life, where he has had close association
with individuals engaged in gambling and illegal activities.
