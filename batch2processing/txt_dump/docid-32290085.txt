WEEKLY SUMMARY

On 6/14/62, NO 1178-C advised SA PERCY V. RICHARDSON
of the Gulfport, Mississippi, RA, that during the early evening
hours of 6/13/62 members of the Mississippi National Guard raided
three gambling establishments on the Mississippi Gulf Coast betwee
Gulfport and Biloxi. The informant identified these three places
as the Gay Paree and the Key Club, located near Biloxi, and a
club known as The Spot in Mississippi City. Liquor and gambling
equipment valued at an estimated $25, 000 were seized and destroyed
in these three raids. :
