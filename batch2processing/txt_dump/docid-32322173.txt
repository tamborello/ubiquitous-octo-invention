Title: JOHN F. KENNEDY RECORDS COLLECTION
ACT OF 1992 (The Act)

Synopsis: This communication sets forth the search results of
the Pittsburgh office for records pertaining to the John F.
Kennedy Assassination.

Administrative: Reference is made to FBIHQ electronic
OC) communication (EC) dated 07/08/1998; and PG telcall this date.

Enclosures: 1) Pittsburgh file 62-3721;
2) search slips;
3) indices cards.

Details: A comprehensive search was conducted by the Pittsburgh
office to locate any materials pertaining to the Kennedy
Assassination. Pittsburgh’s searches which were limited to
records of this Division included the automated indices, manual
indices, elsur indices, confidential indices, and Intelligence
Integrated Information Application (IIIA). A CLEA search was not
conducted by the Pittsburgh office.

Based upon this search, Pittsburgh located file 62-3721
which was not previously reported to FBIHQ. This file is
captioned “ASSASSINATION OF PRESIDENT JOHN F. KENNEDY, DALLAS,
TEXAS, NOVEMBER 22, 1963, MISCELLANEOUS - INFORMATION CONCERNING,
0O: DALLAS.” An indices card which was prepared 11/83,
captioned “THE KENNEDY ASSASSINATION AND BEYOND (PART 1)” was
also located. This card contains a reference to a law
enforcement bulletin (LEB) article #11, Volume 52, November,
1983. The Pittsburgh office only maintains file copies of the
LEB dating back to 1991.
