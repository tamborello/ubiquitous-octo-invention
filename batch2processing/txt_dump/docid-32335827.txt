* The following information was furnished on August 27,

Dallas T-1 advised that he recently had heard that
there were two meetings of the above organization held at the
home of Delbert Ray in Midlothian, Texas. Dallas T-1 stated he
had not personally attended the meetings and, therefore, did not
know how many people were present or the dates the meetings were
held. He said he had been told that Otto (Duke) Koling had :
attended both of the meetings.

 

Dallas T-1 related he heard that the discussion which
developed at these meetings concerned the recent racial riots
which occurred in‘Los Angeles, California, and the possibility
that such riots might occur in the Dallas, Texas, area. Dallas
T-1 related that he did not know at present if any specific plans.
had been formulated concerning what steps might be taken by
captioned organization should similar riots occur in the Dallas,
Texas, area.

Dallas T-1 advised that other than the above meetings,
he knew of no presentplans of the group, but said he would remain
alert for such information.
