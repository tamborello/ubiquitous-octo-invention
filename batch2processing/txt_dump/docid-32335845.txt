‘The following information was furnished on
December 1, 1965, by Dallas T-1:
Dallas T-1 advised that on Thanksgiving evening,
November 25, 1965, OTTO "DUKE" KOLING, an individual, who
had in the past been associated with captioned organization,
had been killed in a motorcycle accident at Ft. Worth, Texas.

Dallas T-1 advised that he had not heard of any
meetings held by the American Underground,and in his opinion
the organization seemed to be dying out. He advised that as
OTTO “DUKE’ KOLING had been one of the main contacts in the —
organization, his recent death would be an additional reason
for lack of activity by anyone who had in the past been
associated with the group.
