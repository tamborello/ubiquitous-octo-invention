name used by members of the organized Italian criminal element
to refer to their over-all criminal organization, The framework
of "La Cosa Nostra" consists of "borgate" or "families", each
ruled by a "capo" or "boss", The "capo" appoints a "sotto-capo"
or "underboss", arid a "consiglieri" or "counselor", who advises
the "boss" and the "soldati" or "soldiers", when required.

The “soldati" are the Yowest strata of membership and are
Girectly under a "capodecina" (a "captain" or "“lieutenant"),
appointed by the "capo" to exercise his authority over the
"soldati" assigned to a "decina", or group. A "Commissione"
or "Commission", comprised of a group of "bosses" from various
parts of the United States is the ultimate authority of "La
Cosa Nostra". Members of the "Cemmissione"are referred to
individuaily as "pappresentante", a term also signifying the
status of "boss",
