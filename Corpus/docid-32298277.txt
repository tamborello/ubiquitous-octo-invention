FRANK COPPOLA, referred to by the Milwaukee
informant is identical with FRANCISCO COPPOLA, who was
deported from the United States in 1947. COPPOLA was
born in 1899 at Partinico, Sicily. He entered the
United States in 1926 and was deported in 1947.

; He returned in 1948 and was again deported in
1949. He was arrested on 8/3/65, at Palermo, Sicily by
Italian Police and was described as a Mafia leader in
Italy.

 

During the time that he resided in the United
States:‘he was known to be a close associate of CARLOS : .
MARCELLO of New Orleans, Louisiana. I

COPPOLA resided in Detroit from about 1931
to 1944. He is believed to have organized the Lucky
Star Numbers House and became extremely wealthy and
influencial. He was very close to JOHN PRIZIOLA and
RAFFAELE QUASARANO, aka Jimmy Q.

These latter two individuals are reportedly
members of the Detroit LCN family who were engaged over
the years in narcotics traffic.
