ANTI-RACKETEERING

Reference is made to the report of Special Agent
Thomas G. Forsyth, III, Pittsburgh, dated September 30, 1963.

There follows a brief characterization of informants
used in referenced report.

PG T-24 is an individual who has operated
gambling establishments, horse rooms, and numbers stations
in the Allegheny River Valley Section of Western Pennsyl-
vania for almost 20 years.

PG T-32 is a businessman in Mercer County, Pa.,
who has a life-time association with gamblers and racketeers.

PG T-39 is a gambler and club operator in
Pittsburgh, Pa.

PG T-52 is a law enforcement officer whose duties
take him to most parts of Western Pennsylvania.

PG T-53 is an individual who has had many years
experience in the numbers racket and other forms of gambling
in the Monongahela Valley Section of Western Pennsylvania.
