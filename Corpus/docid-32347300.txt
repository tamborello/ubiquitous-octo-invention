SUBJECT: Request for Information on Carlos MARCELLO (U)
REFERENCE: A. OLC 77-5528 dated 9 December 1977

B. SA/DO/O Memo dated 21 December 1977

1. (S) An intensive search of the files and indices
of this Directorate reveals no indication that this Agency
possesses any CIA material of any nature whatsoever on the
above referenced individual or has had any contact of any
nature whatsoever with him. This search, however, has
revealed three Drug Enforcement Administration documents
dated 1971, 1973, and 1976, which refer to one Carlos
Joseph MARCELLO (born 6 February 1910) of Mew Grleans,
Louisiana, who was involved in organized crime and drug
smuggling. :

2. (U) It has been further determined that this
individual has not been the Subject of an OGC or FOIA name
trace request.

3. (U) This information was conveyed to Ms. June
. Legleiter of your office by telephone on 27 December 1977

at 1525 hours.
