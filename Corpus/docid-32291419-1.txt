On 2/20/63, following their arrest by Dallas Police
Officers, on charges of bookmaking, and on information
furnished the PD by the Dallas Office, MARVIN C. COX and
SHERMAN FRANKLIN LITTLE were interviewed by Bureau Agents.
‘COX is a runner for LITTLE's bookmaking operation in Dallas,
and LITTLE is the subject of Dallas file 162-129, captioned
“SHERMAN FRANKLIN LITTLE, aka., IGA," Dallas origin.

Both men were very uncooperative and non-committal
and refused to discuss their bookmaking activities whatsoever.
In fact both disdaimed any such activity on their parts.

On 2/28/63, JAMES CHILTON, subject of Dallas

file 165-103 was interviewed by Bureau Agents at his used
car lot, Dallas. CHILTON was very cordial and appeared to
be cooperative, He admitted acquaintanceship with numerous
Dallas area gamblers and bookmakers, and furnished pertinent
background information as to gambling in the Dallas area.
prior to the interview no information had been developed
that CHILTON was in violation of any Federal anti-gambling
statutes. The ITWI investigation has been discontinued and

a new 137 case re CHILTON, has been opened by the Dallas Office.
