DN 100-1800

Re: Stop the War Committee 1

As of. August, 1966, the Denver "Stop the War
Committee"" had been holding meetings and sponsoring
demonstrations in Denver, Colorado, protesting
United States policy in Vietnam. This committee
is an ad hoc type committee utilized to lend a
sponsor name to demonstrations. MARY WALTER is
chairman of the committee and various members of
the Denver Branch, Socialist Workers Party (SWP),
are generally the guiding figures in all Vietnam
demonstrations.

MARY WALTER was secretary of the Denver
Chapter, Fair Play for Cuba Committee (FPCC) .

DN 215-s.
8/16/66

Characterizations of the Denver Branch,
SWP, md the Denver Chapter, FPCC, appear
as appendices hereto.

On February 16, 1968, DN 215-S advised that no action i
has been taken by this committee at this time.

New Orleans will be kept advised of any activities
in the Denver Division on behalf of Mr. GARRISON.
