On 3/13/64 PH 216-S advised that THOMAS NABRIED and
FRANCES GABOW attended a meeting of the Philadelphia-Friends of
the National Guardian on 3/11/64. MARK LANE, attorney for the
mother of LEE HARVEY OSWALD, accused assassin of President Kennedy,
was the main speaker. He commented on the five-page brief which
he filed on behalf of OSWALD, His main point was that there is
some doubt as to OSWALD's guilt. LANE also said that a suitable
defense could be fashioned for him.

NABRIED, according to the informant, mentioned that
he noticed many old time Party members and Party sympathizers
at this meeting. He said these people seem to be assoclated
with the Philadelphia Friends of the National Guardian. NABRIED
also noticed STERLING ROCHESTER, CHARLES ROBINSON, AL and BEA
KATZ, DAVE and SOFIE DAVIS and others too numerous to recall.
