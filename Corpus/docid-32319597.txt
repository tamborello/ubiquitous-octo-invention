Based on data furnished by the Records Section and compiled
by the Executive Branch Agencies Unit (EBAU), 190,870 name check communi-
cations falling within the responsibilities of this unit were received
during the month of April, 1979, and a total of 170,360 (including
pending submissions carried over from the previous month) were
processed. A total of 32,941 forms, as well as 595 telephonic requests,
were directly handled by review clerks and analysts of the EBAU. In
connection with this work, 8,387 files were received for review purposes;
957 copies of reports were disseminated to appropriate agencies; 1,281
file references were made; and 138 memoranda were prepared by analysts.
A total of. 27,612 checks were determined to be "No Record." The
remaining name checks relating to the work of the EBAU which were
processed during the month and determined in the Records Section to be
"No Record" were processed by this Unit for transmittal to the
submitting agencies.

Enclosed are copies of more specific (by requester) breakdowns
of the work of the EBAU for April, 1979.
